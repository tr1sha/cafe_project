S O C A and J A M is a fresh take on Caribbean Chic, Breakfast and Brunch!

Get together with friends and family in our lovely, sleek,botanical-infused location, where you can enjoy breakfast or brunch tapas in a rejuvinating atmosphere.


We offer up traditional Caribbean morning faves with a modern twist, as well as Continental options sure to please!
Choose your Dish du Jour from our delicious menu; all of our dishes and pastries are made fresh, inhouse, and ingredients sourced through local channels.
Marley Coffee® Jamaica Blue Mountain® Coffee are our coffee partners- producers, known for their sustainable and conscious cultiavtion practices, not to mention outstanding coffee, that serves to adding an exquisit Caribbean experience to your time with us. For the health-minded to the indulgent, you are sure to find something to suit your mood!
Our motto here is: "Vivacious Food!" as we do our best to serve quality, healthy and beautiful plates on every occasion.

We also cater and host large parties, from a family brunch to special events and we would be happy to accomodate your group.
